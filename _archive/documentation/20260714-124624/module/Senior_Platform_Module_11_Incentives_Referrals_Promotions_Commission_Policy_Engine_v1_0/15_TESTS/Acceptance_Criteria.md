# Acceptance Criteria

1. Campaigns can be created, versioned, activated, paused and archived.
2. Published policy versions are immutable.
3. Referrals can be registered through code/link/manual attribution.
4. Self-referral is blocked when configured.
5. Rewards are created as pending, not paid.
6. Order completion alone does not guarantee reward payout.
7. Settlement requires financial, dispute and fraud criteria.
8. Commission reductions are evaluated by policy and settled by Module 05.
9. Historical evaluations retain policy version.
10. Tenant data cannot be crossed.
11. Every reward decision is explainable.
12. Reversal creates events and financial reversal instructions.
13. Disabled campaigns stop new evaluations but preserve history.
14. Fraud holds prevent payout until resolved.
15. Reports can break down campaign performance by policy version.
