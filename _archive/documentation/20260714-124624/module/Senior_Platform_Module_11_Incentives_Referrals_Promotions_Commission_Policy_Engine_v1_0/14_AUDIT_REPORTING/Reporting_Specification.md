# Reporting Specification

## Reports
- campaign ROI
- campaign spend
- pending rewards
- payable rewards
- paid rewards
- reversed rewards
- expired rewards
- referral conversion rate
- signup conversion by channel
- provider activation rate
- customer acquisition cost
- lifetime value by campaign
- fraud hold rate
- best referrers
- best performing geographies
- commission reduction impact

## Dimensions
tenant, company, actor role, segment, city, region, service category, campaign, policy version, time window, referral channel, risk level.
