# Glossary

## Customer or Customer Delegate
The person or family member requesting and selecting care services.

## Independent Provider
Independent provider not operating under a company for the selected service.

## Organization Provider
Provider affiliated with a company.

## Organization
Organization offering services directly or through company providers.

## Platform Owner
Platform owner and highest-level administrator.

## Request
A service request created by Module 01.

## Service Need
A specific service requirement inside a request.

## Eligibility
Whether a provider is allowed to receive and respond to a request.

## Fitness
How well an eligible candidate fits the request.

## Matching
Generating possible candidate options.

## Ranking
Ordering candidates for presentation.

## Recommendation
Explained suggestion to help customer/family decide.

## Candidate
A matching option.

## Accepted Candidate
Candidate that has accepted and can be shown to customer/family.

## Selection Lock
Technical record that customer/family selected one candidate for a service need or package.

## Broadcast
Distribution strategy where all eligible providers receive the request.

## Wave-Based Distribution
Future strategy where request is sent in batches.

## Smart Distribution
Future strategy where system/AI selects whom to notify.

## Manual Intervention
Human support action in Matching.

## Audit Log
Permanent record of manual/system decision.
