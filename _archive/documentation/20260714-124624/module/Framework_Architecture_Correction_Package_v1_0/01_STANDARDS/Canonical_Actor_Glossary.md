# Canonical Actor Glossary v1.0

| Canonical Actor | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Platform Owner | Legal/commercial owner of the marketplace platform. |
| Platform Team Member | Internal platform staff with delegated permissions. |
| Organization | A provider-side company, agency, studio, clinic, contractor group, or managed supplier entity. |
| Organization Staff | Staff users operating inside an organization with scoped permissions. |
| Independent Provider | A provider acting directly without organization affiliation. |
| Organization Provider | A provider affiliated with an organization after approval. |
| Customer | Person or entity requesting or buying a service. |
| Customer Delegate | A person acting on behalf of the customer account. |
| Trusted Person | Order-scoped person with limited temporary visibility. |
| Public Guest | Non-authenticated or not-yet-registered visitor. |
| System Actor | Automated internal service or scheduled process. |
| External Integration | Third-party provider, payment gateway, identity provider, communication provider, webhook consumer, or partner API. |

Domain-specific names such as patient, nurse, provider, technician, cleaner, driver, tutor, barber, or doctor are reference-implementation aliases only.
