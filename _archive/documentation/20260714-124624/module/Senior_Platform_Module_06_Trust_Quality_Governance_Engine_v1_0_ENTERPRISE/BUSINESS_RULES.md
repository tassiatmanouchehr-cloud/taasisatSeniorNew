# Module 06 — Business Rules v1.0

## General

BR-06-001 — Every governance action must be auditable.  
BR-06-002 — Every Complaint, Dispute, Violation, Risk or Compliance issue must attach to TrustCase.  
BR-06-003 — Signal alone cannot produce punishment.  
BR-06-004 — Case alone cannot produce punishment.  
BR-06-005 — Punishment requires CaseDecision.  
BR-06-006 — Major enforcement should be appealable unless critical safety or confirmed fraud requires immediate lock.

## Review

BR-06-010 — Only parties connected to a completed booking may submit review.  
BR-06-011 — Negative review must not be removed merely because it is negative.  
BR-06-012 — Review removal requires policy violation.  
BR-06-013 — Rating alone must not determine account health.  
BR-06-014 — Rating must be combined with review count, recency, complaint rate, completion history and violation history.

## Complaint & Dispute

BR-06-020 — Complaint is informal/simple; Dispute is formal and decision-oriented.  
BR-06-021 — Every Dispute requires evidence collection.  
BR-06-022 — Financial dispute produces recommendation to Module 05, not direct financial action.  
BR-06-023 — Heavy decision must not rely only on allegation without evidence.

## Violation & Enforcement

BR-06-030 — Violation categories include behavior, safety, financial, fraud, quality and compliance.  
BR-06-031 — Warning, Restriction, Suspension and Ban require CaseDecision.  
BR-06-032 — Restriction must define the exact restricted capability.  
BR-06-033 — Suspension must include reason, start, end and appeal policy.  
BR-06-034 — PermanentBan requires Platform Owner approval.

## Compliance

BR-06-040 — Provider or Organization may be blocked from assignment without required compliance.  
BR-06-041 — Expired compliance must generate RiskSignal or ComplianceCase.  
BR-06-042 — VerificationDocument must not be physically deleted; use revoked, expired or superseded.

## Risk

BR-06-050 — RiskSignal is analytical, not enforcement.  
BR-06-051 — RiskScore must be explainable.  
BR-06-052 — Risk rules must be configurable, versioned and auditable.  
BR-06-053 — repeated cancellation, complaint, refund, location anomaly and suspicious review should generate RiskSignal.
