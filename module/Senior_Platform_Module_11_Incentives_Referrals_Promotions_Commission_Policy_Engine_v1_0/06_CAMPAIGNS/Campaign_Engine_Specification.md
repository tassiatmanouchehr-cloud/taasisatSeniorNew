# Campaign Engine Specification

## Campaign Types
- Referral campaign
- Signup bonus campaign
- Provider acquisition campaign
- Customer acquisition campaign
- Reduced commission campaign
- Cashback campaign
- Coupon campaign
- Geographic launch campaign
- Service category campaign
- Loyalty campaign
- Retention campaign

## Campaign Controls
- start and end dates
- budget cap
- daily cap
- monthly cap
- per-actor cap
- tenant scope
- segment scope
- geographic scope
- service scope
- role scope
- priority
- stacking mode
- approval workflow

## Publication Rules
Campaigns require validation before publication. A campaign cannot publish without at least one policy version, targeting scope, budget behavior, and audit owner.
