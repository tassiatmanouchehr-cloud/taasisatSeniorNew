# Module 11 — Executive Overview

Module 11 is the policy-controlled growth, incentive, referral, promotion, and commission policy engine for a generic service marketplace. It enables marketplace operators to define, activate, deactivate, segment, version, audit, and settle financial or non-financial incentives without modifying order, financial, identity, or booking logic.

## Business Capabilities
- Referral programs for any eligible actor.
- Signup incentives for invitee, inviter, or both.
- Reduced commission for new or targeted providers.
- Campaign-based promotions and discounts.
- Referral earnings from early orders of invited actors.
- Time-limited, geography-limited, tenant-limited, role-limited, or segment-limited offers.
- Budget-controlled campaigns.
- Pending, qualified, earned, payable, paid, reversed, expired and cancelled reward states.
- Fraud-aware incentive qualification.
- Ledger-safe settlement integration.

## Non-Negotiable Rules
1. No reward is final at creation time.
2. Every reward must be explainable.
3. Every evaluation must reference a policy version.
4. Financial effects must be posted through Module 05.
5. Fraud and dispute states must be checked before finalization.
6. Tenant boundaries must never be crossed.
7. Campaign changes must not mutate historical calculations.
